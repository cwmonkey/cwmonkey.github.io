Drag the link to your shortcuts bar:

Clean Stream

When you are on a page with a stream (or any flash player) click the bookmarklet and it will remove everything that is not a flash player from the page.

This doesn't work 100% of the time, but if it messes the page up all you have to do is refresh.

Update: Now works with the WWE Network player.
